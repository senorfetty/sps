About Us

SPS Security (formely Shalom) is now operating as merger of the following companies Shalom SPS Company Ltd ;This comprises of Seamate, Shalom, Perfect Scan forming SPS. We are licensed by Private Security Regulatory Authority PSRA/SSCL/19/Y5, bonded, and fully insured against all types of risks

What You Need To Know About Us

SPS Security is a leading security service organization which operates responsibly drawing upon its vast experience, expertise and human resources while providing maximum level of security for your business, staff, clients and valuables. It is of fundamental importance that the relationships built throughout the company’s dealings are based on trust. Furthermore, the company strives to methodically and systematically invest in top quality, state-of-the-art technology as well as to apply all relevant standards and specifications, in order to fully meet the needs and expectations of its customers.

The partners of SPS security possesses over 20 years’ experience in security, investigations, and the protection services in the field.
